# Privacy Policy

**Email Intelligence Filter - Privacy Policy**

**Last Updated:** April 2026

## 1. Overview

Email Intelligence Filter ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our email analysis service.

## 2. Information We Access

When you connect your Gmail account to Email Intelligence Filter:
- We access **only your email messages** to analyze them for spam detection
- We access **only the emails you authorize** through the OAuth 2.0 consent process
- We **do not** have access to your Google Drive, Calendar, or other Google services

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your emails are used **exclusively for:**
- Analyzing spam detection patterns
- Categorizing emails (Work, Promotional, Academic)
- Identifying important emails based on your keywords
- Providing analysis results to you in real-time

## 4. Data Storage

- **During Session:** Your emails are analyzed in memory during your active session
- **After Session:** No emails are permanently stored on our servers
- **Automatic Deletion:** All session data is deleted when you log out or close the app
- **No Backups:** We do not create backups of your email data

## 5. Data Sharing

- **Third Parties:** We do NOT share your email data with any third parties
- **Google:** We use Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol - Google does NOT share your emails with us directly; you authorize us to access them
- **Analytics:** We do NOT use any analytics tools that track your emails
- **Advertising:** We do NOT use your data for advertising

## 6. Security

- **Encryption:** All communication between the app and Google is encrypted (HTTPS)
- **OAuth 2.0:** We use industry-standard OAuth 2.0 authentication
- **No Credentials Stored:** We do not save your Google password or access tokens
- **Local Processing:** Your emails are processed locally; they are never sent to external servers

## 7. Your Rights

You have the following rights:
- **Disconnect Anytime:** You can disconnect Gmail access at any time by clicking "Logout"
- **Revoke Access:** You can revoke the app's access from your [Google Account Security](https://myaccount.google.com/security-checkup)
- **No Data Request:** Since we don't store data, there's nothing to export or delete

## 8. Age Restriction

Email Intelligence Filter is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
